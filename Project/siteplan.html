Food Recipe Sharing Website Plan

Recipe 4 All

Recipe 4 All: Recipe 4all

This name reflects a platform where users can explore, share, and enjoy a variety of recipes and food ideas from around the world.

Optional Domain: recipe4all.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a space for food enthusiasts to share their favorite recipes, discover new dishes, and stay updated on trending meals and delicacies.

Scenarios

  • How can I share my recipe with the community?
  • What are the trending meals this season?
  • Where can I find unique recipes for special occasions?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #A62349 (Used for headings and buttons)

Accent Color: #F89C1C (Used for highlights and accents)

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display, serif (Used for headings)

Body Font: Roboto, sans-serif (Used for general text)
